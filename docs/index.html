About This Project

Why we created this collection and how it can help you

Our Mission

Testing AI/ML systems presents unique challenges beyond traditional software testing. This curated list provides resources for ensuring the quality, reliability, and fairness of your AI/ML systems throughout their lifecycle.

Whether you're new to ML testing or an experienced practitioner, this list offers valuable resources for implementing effective testing strategies for your AI/ML applications.

Project Focus

We focus on gathering and organizing the most relevant and effective tools, frameworks, and resources for testing AI/ML systems across different domains and applications. Our goal is to help practitioners navigate the complex landscape of AI/ML testing and find the right solutions for their specific needs.

This project emphasizes practical, battle-tested resources that have proven value in real-world AI/ML testing scenarios, from data validation to model evaluation and monitoring.

Community-Driven

Maintained by the community, for the community. We welcome contributions from all practitioners.

Curated Quality

Each resource is carefully vetted to ensure relevance and value to AI/ML testing practitioners.

Comprehensive Coverage

From general frameworks to specialized tools for fairness testing, we cover the full spectrum.

Constantly Evolving

The list is regularly updated with new tools and resources as the field evolves.
